Who We Are

The Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT) at Kennesaw State University is a vibrant engine of discovery—where passion, purpose, and partnership converge to advance the ever-evolving world of information technology. Fueled by the conviction that research reaches its full potential when it sparks real change, CARIT unites a dynamic team of faculty visionaries, graduate innovators, and undergraduate trailblazers. Together, we transform big ideas into tangible impact, working tirelessly at the crossroads of theory and action to deliver meaningful value for every person and organization we serve.

Our Mission & Values

CARIT’s mission pulses with energy: to drive applied IT research that forges powerful connections between Kennesaw State University and the vibrant business and civic community. Guided by unwavering values of integrity, collaboration, inclusivity, and excellence, we infuse every initiative with passion and purpose. We know the most extraordinary solutions are born when diverse voices unite, and we relentlessly pursue partnerships that celebrate the dynamic spirit of the Greater Atlanta region. From cybersecurity and data science to software development and IT consulting, our bold work ignites progress across the entire information technology landscape.

Our Faculty

CARIT is fueled by a team of passionate faculty leaders whose expertise electrifies every corner of the information technology landscape. Our researchers blend years of academic achievement with real-world industry know-how, keeping our work not just relevant, but groundbreaking and future-focused. Every faculty member is an inspiring mentor, an energetic innovator, and a driving force in forging powerful partnerships with students and the community alike.
