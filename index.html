What is CARIT

The Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT) at Kennesaw State University crackles with energy, sparking bold connections between academic innovation and the fast-paced world of technology. Fueled by a passion for transformative collaboration, CARIT rallies visionary faculty, driven graduate students, and dynamic industry leaders to take on the greatest challenges in information technology. Our mission is to unleash breakthrough research, channeling it into energetic, real-world solutions that inspire and empower businesses, organizations, and the vibrant Georgia community.

Our Focus ~ Our Community

CARIT thrives at the cutting edge of applied research, blazing dynamic trails across information technology fields—from cybersecurity and data analytics to software engineering and emerging tech. What truly sets us apart is our contagious enthusiasm and relentless drive to meet the real needs of our community. Through spirited partnerships with local businesses and public organizations, we deliver innovative, actionable solutions that make an immediate and lasting impact. We don’t just bridge the gap between ideas and industry—we energize it, propelling progress with unstoppable passion.

Become part of our passionate, energetic community that's not just shaping the future of IT, but leading it with vision, enthusiasm, and unstoppable momentum.
